About

In these days of mass production, it is rare to find a bakery that devotes itself to quality over quantity.   Cookie Dough uses only quality ingredients.  All of our cookies and cakes are made from scratch with the finest ingredients, providing you with cookies and cakes of the highest quality.  Our goal is to be the pioneers in the preparation of cookies in the country. Perfecting the quality every day to guarantee a moment of magic consumption to our customers.  Cookie Dough is a family-owned business, serving Ann Arbor since 1974, and is now at Canton.
